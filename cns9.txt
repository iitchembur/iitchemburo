1. Ipconfig and get the ip address of the PC and put it in the target.

2. Default Quick Scan : nmap  192.168.29.19
3. Scan of first 200 ports: nmap -p 1-200 192.168.29.19
4. Fast Scan of top 100 ports:  nmap -F 192.168.29.19

5. TCP connect() scan: nmap -sT -p 1-200 192.168.29.19
6.  UDP scan common ports : nmap -sU 192.168.29.19
7. Identify services & versions on common ports: nmap -sV -p 1-200 192.168.29.19
8. OS Fingerprinting:  nmap -O 192.168.29.19
9. Aggressive Scan (-A): nmap -A 192.168.29.19
10. Find live hosts on your /24 subnet: nmap -sn 192.168.29.19/24
11. HTTP title (safe): nmap -sV -p 80,8080 --script http-title 192.168.29.19
